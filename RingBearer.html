Let's Design a Jewelry Holder!

Plastic Halo "Ring Bearer"

The Concept

I aimed to design a product for Plastic Halo that showcases the brand's themes in a functional and playful manner, all while exuding a sense of elegance.

3D Design

Let's get to modeling!

The Prototype

I 3D printed two different sizes, where the small one holds rings loosely while the larger one has more of a snug fit. I experimented with a special epoxy glaze that would seal the paint and make it more heat resistant if ever used as an ashtray. I made sure to use a compound with no harmful chemicals or VOCs.

The Product

Although I only made 8 of these using the mold I have created, the Ring Bearer seems to catch a lot of attention from friends and family who visit my home/studio!
